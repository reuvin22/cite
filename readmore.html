HISTORY


The College of Information Technology
Education started on.




In 1991 the College of Information Technology Education offered Two-Year Computer Science Course.
The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science was offered under the College of Business Administration
in 1993. In 2003 the Supervision/Management of the Computer Science Department was transferred from
the College of Business Administration and Accountancy to College of Environmental Design and Engineering.
In 2004 B.S. in Information Technology and B.S. in Information Management programs has been offered.In 2008
the School of Computer Studies (SCS) has been established. In the year 2009 the name of the department has
been changed from SCS to School of Information Technology Education. And from School of Information
Technology Education it has been changed to College of Information Technology Education in the year 2015.


HISTORY OF GOVERNMENT RECOGNITION




It was in the year 1996 where the Government gave recognition to the
Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Program and in 2011
for Bachelor of Science in Information Technology Program



COD




In 2016, The College of Information Technology Education was recognized
as one of the two schools in Region 3 and First in Bulacan as Center of Development
for Information Technology Education. This recognition was given by the Commission
on Higher Education through the recommendation of the Technical Panel in Information
Technology Education.
